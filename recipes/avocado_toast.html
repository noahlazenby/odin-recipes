Avocado Toast

Description: Toasted sourdough topped with whipped avocado and a fried egg

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • Ripe avocado
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon

Instructions

  1. Prepare your avocado spread by mixing 1 avocado, 2 tbsp lemon juice and salt and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Toast the sourdough bread to your liking.
  3. Heat your skillet to medium and add some oil or butter. Fry your egg to your liking
  4. Once your bread is toasted, spread your avocado mixture and top with your fried egg.
  5. Enjoy with your favorite beverage!
