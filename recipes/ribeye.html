Skillet Seared Ribeye

Description: Perfectly cooked medium rare ribeye finished with grass fed butter and thyme.

Ingredients

  • Grass-fed, grass-finished Ribeye
  • Grass-fed butter
  • Thyme
  • Fresh garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: Beef tallow

Instructions

  1. Pat dry your steak and generously coat each side with salt and pepper. Let rest for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a cast iron or non-stick skillet on high heat.
  3. Add 1 tbsp of beef tallow or butter to the hot pan. Let it melt and coat entire skillet.
  4. Once the pan is hot and coated with your tallow/butter, add the steak. Make sure the steak is flat and is making good contact with the skillet.
  5. Depending on the thickness of the steak and how you like it prepared, cook for 2-4 minutes on each side.
  6. After cooking on each side, reduce the heat to low and add in your butter and thyme to your skillet.
  7. Begin to butter baste the steak for 1-2 minutes on each side.
  8. Remove the steak from the skillet and let rest at room temperature for 5-10 minutes.
  9. Now enjoy with your favorite side dishes!
